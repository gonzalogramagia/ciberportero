## Simulating the Real World

I have embarked on a new challenge: building a complete **simulated virtual network** using **Cisco Packet Tracer**. My goal is to apply the networking concepts I am learning through the **Cisco Networking Academy** courses in a practical, hands-on environment.

This blog post will serve as a living document of my progress, where I will share the topologies I design, the configurations I implement, and the troubleshooting steps I take.

## Why Cisco Packet Tracer?

Packet Tracer is a powerful network simulation tool that allows students to experiment with network behavior and ask "what if" questions. It is an essential tool for anyone preparing for Cisco certifications (like CCNA) or simply wanting to understand how networks operate without the need for expensive physical hardware.
